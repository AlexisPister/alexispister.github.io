Presentation

Hello, I am Alexis Pister. I am a second year PhD in the fields of visualization and data mining applied to historical social networks at University Paris-Saclay , more precisely at Inria Saclay (Aviz team) and Telecom Paris, under the supervision of Jean-Daniel Fekete and Christophe Prieur.

My subject consists in developing visualization and analysis tools for community detection and pattern mining in historical dynamical social networks, with a strong focus on explainability and interactions.
